Bird of Paradise
Watering Frequency:

Every 1-2 weeks, allowing the top 2-3" of the soil to become dry between waterings.

Light Preference:

Bright, direct sunlight

Repot:

Every 2-3 years, or when it becomes root-bound.

Benefits:

Air circulation and purification.

Pet Toxicity:

Toxic to both cats and dogs.

Monstera
Watering Frequency:

Every 1-2 weeks, allowing the soil to dry out between waterings.

Light Preference:

Bright to medium-bright indirect light.

Repot:

Every 1-2 years, or when it becomes root bound.

Benefits:

Air purification and maintain humidity.

Pet Toxicity:

Toxic to both cats and dogs.

Dancing-lady Orchid
Watering Frequency:

Every 2-10 days, when the planting media is half dry.

Light Preference:

Bright to full direct sunlight.

Repot:

Every 1-2 years, or when it becomes root bound.

Benefits:

Air purification and freshening.

Pet Toxicity:

Non-toxic to both cats and dogs.

Alocasia
Watering Frequency:

Every week in the summer, and allowing 2" of soil to dry between waterings in the winter.

Light Preference:

Bright indirect light.

Repot:

Every other year, or when it becomes root bound.

Benefits:

Air purification and stress reduction.

Pet Toxicity:

Toxic to both cats and dogs.
